# Persona: Sales Representative

## Profile
- **Name:** Daniel Foster
- **Age:** 34
- **Role:** Senior Sales Executive
- **Company Type:** Professional Services Firm
- **Experience:** 9 years in B2B sales
- **Location:** Birmingham, UK

## Background
Daniel is on the front lines, having conversations with prospects daily. He uses the website as a tool to educate prospects, share resources, and overcome objections. He needs materials that make his job easier and help close deals faster.

## Goals & Motivations
- Close more deals faster
- Overcome prospect objections effectively
- Educate prospects about services
- Demonstrate value and ROI
- Differentiate from competitors
- Hit quota and earn commission

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Prospects who don't understand the value
- Long sales cycles with multiple stakeholders
- Objections about price or implementation
- Competing against established competitors
- Lack of sales enablement materials
- Website that doesn't support sales conversations
- Difficulty getting prospects to take next step
- Marketing materials that don't address real objections

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Sales Enablement:** Presentations, one-pagers, battlecards
- **Case Studies:** Specific, relevant customer success stories
- **Comparison Content:** How you compare to competitors
- **Objection Handling:** Content that addresses common concerns
- **ROI Calculators:** Tools to demonstrate financial value
- **Service Details:** Deep information about what's included
- **Pricing Guidance:** At least directional pricing information
- **Testimonials:** Quotes and video testimonials from happy customers
- **Easy Sharing:** Resources formatted for easy sending to prospects
- **Demo Materials:** Videos or interactive demos
- **Industry-Specific Content:** Tailored messaging by sector
- **Trust Signals:** Awards, certifications, client logos

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Buying Process:** How does each persona buy?
- **Decision Criteria:** What matters most to them?
- **Objections:** What concerns do they raise?
- **Stakeholders:** Who else is involved in the decision?
- **Sales Cycle Length:** How long does it take to close?
- **Deal Size:** What's the typical contract value?
- **Win Rate:** Which personas convert best?
- **Competitor Comparisons:** Who are they considering?
- **Qualifying Questions:** What determines if they're a good fit?
- **Pain Points:** What problems are they trying to solve?

## Technical Proficiency
- Expert: Sales process, negotiation, relationship building, CRM usage
- Advanced: Sales presentations, objection handling, consultative selling
- Intermediate: Business acumen, basic marketing concepts
- Tools: Salesforce, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, email, presentation software

## Preferred Communication Channels
- Sales playbooks and battlecards
- Case studies and success stories

- Email templates and scripts

## Key Quotes
> "The website should pre-sell prospects before I even talk to them."

> "I don't need more leads. I need better-qualified leads who understand our value."

> "Give me materials I can send to prospects that address their specific concerns."

## Red Flags for This Persona
- Generic, unhelpful content
- No sales enablement materials
- Missing case studies or examples
- No competitive differentiation
- Lack of pricing guidance
- No objection-handling content
- Resources that aren't easy to share
- No industry-specific messaging
- Missing trust signals
- Complex, confusing service descriptions
- No demo or proof of capabilities

- [ ] Are sales enablement materials available?
- [ ] Are case studies specific and relevant?
- [ ] Is competitive differentiation clear?
- [ ] Is there content addressing common objections?
- [ ] Are ROI tools or calculators available?
- [ ] Are service details comprehensive?
- [ ] Is pricing guidance provided?

- [ ] Are resources easily shareable?
- [ ] Are there demo videos or materials?
- [ ] Is there industry-specific content?

- [ ] Can prospects self-qualify?
