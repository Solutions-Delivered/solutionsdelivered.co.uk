# Persona: Existing Customer

## Profile
- **Name:** Andrew Morrison
- **Age:** 45
- **Role:** Operations Director
- **Company:** Mid-size manufacturing company
- **Customer Since:** 2 years
- **Location:** Birmingham, UK

## Background
Andrew has been working with Solutions Delivered for 2 years on process improvement and project management initiatives. He's familiar with the company's services and has built relationships with the team. He's a satisfied customer who values the partnership.

## Goals & Motivations
- Get ongoing value from current services
- Explore additional services for new challenges
- Stay informed about new offerings and updates
- Access support when needed
- Justify continued investment to stakeholders
- Build long-term partnership

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Finding information about new services available
- Accessing support or account management
- Understanding billing and contract details
- Keeping stakeholders informed about value delivered
- Coordinating across multiple projects
- Knowing who to contact for different needs

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Customer Portal:** Easy access to account information
- **Support Access:** Clear path to get help quickly
- **Service Updates:** Information about new offerings and updates
- **Case Studies:** Success stories similar to their situation
- **Resources:** Best practices, guides, templates they can use
- **Contact Information:** Direct access to their account team
- **Documentation:** Help articles, FAQs, how-to guides
- **Billing Information:** Clear pricing and invoice access
- **Expansion Opportunities:** Related services they might need
- **Trust Signals:** Continued evidence of expertise and results
- **Community:** Peer connections or customer community
- **News & Updates:** Company news and industry insights

## Red Flags for This Persona
- No clear customer portal or login
- Difficult to find support contact information

- No account management visibility
- Unclear billing or contract information
- No path to explore additional services
- Forgetting about existing customers in marketing
