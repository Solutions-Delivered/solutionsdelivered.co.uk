# Persona: Digital Marketing Strategist

## Profile
- **Name:** Liam O'Brien
- **Age:** 31
- **Role:** Senior Digital Marketing Strategist
- **Company Type:** Digital Marketing Agency
- **Experience:** 8 years in digital marketing, specializing in SEO and conversion optimization
- **Location:** Dublin, Ireland

## Background
Liam develops comprehensive digital marketing strategies for clients across various industries. He's highly technical, understanding how search engines work, how users behave, and what drives conversions. He focuses on data-driven optimization and continuous improvement.

## Goals & Motivations
- Increase organic traffic through SEO best practices
- Improve conversion rates through CRO techniques
- Maximize ROI from paid advertising campaigns
- Create integrated digital marketing strategies
- Stay ahead of algorithm changes and industry trends
- Demonstrate measurable impact on business metrics

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Clients who want quick results from SEO
- Technical SEO issues that developers don't prioritize
- Competing with larger competitors for keywords
- Attribution complexity across multiple channels
- Keeping up with constant algorithm changes
- Balancing short-term wins with long-term strategy

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Technical SEO:** Clean code, semantic HTML, proper meta tags, structured data
- **Core Web Vitals:** Excellent performance scores (LCP, FID, CLS)
- **Mobile-First:** Responsive design optimized for mobile devices
- **Page Speed:** Fast loading times (under 3 seconds)
- **Content Structure:** Proper headings, keyword optimization, internal linking
- **URL Structure:** Clean, descriptive URLs with proper hierarchy
- **XML Sitemap:** Complete and up-to-date sitemap for search engines
- **Schema Markup:** Rich snippets for better SERP appearance
- **Conversion Optimization:** Clear CTAs, reduced friction, persuasive copy
- **Analytics Setup:** Proper tracking, goals, events, e-commerce tracking
- **User Experience Signals:** Low bounce rate, high time on page, pages per session
- **Content Quality:** Comprehensive, valuable content targeting user intent

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Search Behavior:** What keywords and phrases does each persona search for?
- **Search Intent:** Informational, navigational, commercial, or transactional?
- **Content Needs:** What questions or problems are they researching?
- **Device Preferences:** Desktop, mobile, or tablet usage patterns?
- **Geographic Location:** Local, regional, national, or international targeting?
- **Funnel Stage:** Top-of-funnel awareness or bottom-of-funnel conversion content?
- **Engagement Metrics:** Expected bounce rate, time on site, conversion rate by persona?
- **Traffic Potential:** Search volume and competition for persona-related keywords?
- **Conversion Value:** Which personas have the highest conversion potential?

## Red Flags for This Persona
- Poor technical SEO implementation
- Slow page load times or poor Core Web Vitals
- Missing or incorrect meta tags
- No structured data / schema markup
- Thin or duplicate content
- Poor mobile experience
- Missing analytics or improper tracking
- High bounce rates or short time on page
- Difficult conversion process
- No clear keyword targeting strategy
- Missing internal linking structure

## Website Evaluation Checklist
- [ ] Are Core Web Vitals in the "Good" range?
- [ ] Is the site mobile-first and responsive?
- [ ] Are meta titles and descriptions optimized?
- [ ] Is structured data properly implemented?
- [ ] Is the URL structure clean and logical?
- [ ] Are headings used properly (H1, H2, H3)?
- [ ] Is there an XML sitemap?
- [ ] Are images optimized with alt text?
- [ ] Is internal linking strategic and intentional?
- [ ] Are conversion paths clear and frictionless?
- [ ] Is analytics tracking properly implemented?
- [ ] Is content optimized for target keywords?
- [ ] Are there clear CTAs on every page?
- [ ] Is the site HTTPS secure?
- [ ] Are canonical tags implemented correctly?
