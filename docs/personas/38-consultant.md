# Persona: Consultant

## Profile
- **Name:** Alexandra Singh
- **Age:** 41
- **Role:** Independent Management Consultant
- **Specialty:** Business transformation and change management
- **Experience:** 16 years consulting, 8 years independent
- **Location:** Bristol, UK

## Background
Alexandra is an independent consultant who sometimes partners with other firms, refers clients, or competes for the same work. She's well-connected in the consulting community and values professional relationships. She may encounter the company as a potential partner, referral source, or competitor.

## Goals & Motivations
- Build strong professional network
- Find reliable partners for larger projects
- Refer clients to trusted providers
- Stay informed about market players
- Differentiate her own services
- Maintain professional reputation

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Difficult to assess quality of potential partners
- Risk of referring clients to poor providers
- Competitive marketplace
- Finding complementary service providers
- Protecting own client relationships
- Balancing collaboration and competition

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Service Clarity:** Clear understanding of what you do and don't do
- **Complementary Services:** Opportunities for partnership or referral
- **Quality Indicators:** Evidence of professional standards
- **Team Expertise:** Backgrounds and qualifications
- **Client Types:** Who you work with and at what scale
- **Approach & Methodology:** How you work and deliver value
- **Partnership Opportunities:** Information about partnering or subcontracting
- **Reputation Signals:** Client testimonials, case studies, awards
- **Professional Network:** Industry participation and connections
- **Competitive Positioning:** How you position vs. other options
- **Contact Information:** Easy way to reach for business development
- **Thought Leadership:** Insights that demonstrate expertise

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Partnership Fit:** Which personas do both companies serve?
- **Complementary Needs:** Which personas need combined services?
- **Referral Potential:** Which personas could be referred either direction?
- **Competitive Overlap:** Where do you compete for same personas?
- **Specialization:** Which personas do each serve best?
- **Client Value:** Can partnerships deliver more value to personas?
- **Market Positioning:** How do you each position to personas?
- **Collaboration Opportunities:** Where can you work together?

## Red Flags for This Persona
- Vague or unclear service offerings
- Reputation concerns or bad client experiences
- Overlap without clear differentiation
- No partnership or collaboration information
- Poor professionalism or client treatment
- Overpromising or aggressive sales tactics
- No clear expertise or methodology
- Missing quality indicators
- Difficult to reach or poor communication
- Competitive behavior in partnership situations
