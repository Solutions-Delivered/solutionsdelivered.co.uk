# Persona: Social Media Manager

## Profile
- **Name:** Jake Morrison
- **Age:** 28
- **Role:** Social Media Manager
- **Company Type:** Digital Marketing Agency
- **Experience:** 6 years in social media marketing and community management
- **Location:** Brighton, UK

## Background
Jake manages social media presence across multiple platforms, creates shareable content, and drives engagement and traffic. He views the website as both a destination for social traffic and a source of content to share. He understands how social media and websites work together.

## Goals & Motivations
- Drive traffic from social media to website
- Create shareable, engaging content
- Build brand awareness and community
- Increase social engagement and followers
- Support conversion goals through social
- Measure social media ROI

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Content that doesn't perform well socially
- Difficulty tracking social traffic and conversions
- Poor mobile experience for social referrals
- Images that don't display well on social platforms
- Missing or poor Open Graph metadata
- No clear content to share or promote
- Tracking attribution from social channels

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Open Graph Tags:** Proper OG tags for Facebook, LinkedIn
- **Twitter Cards:** Optimized Twitter card metadata
- **Shareable Content:** Blog posts, resources, tools worth sharing
- **Social Share Buttons:** Easy sharing to social platforms
- **Visual Content:** Images, infographics, videos that work on social
- **Mobile Optimization:** Great mobile experience for social traffic
- **UTM Tracking:** Proper campaign tracking from social
- **Social Proof:** Social media follower counts, social feeds
- **Engaging Headlines:** Compelling, shareable headlines
- **Landing Pages:** Dedicated pages for social campaigns
- **Social Integration:** Social feeds embedded on site
- **Fast Loading:** Quick load times for social referrals
- **Clear CTAs:** What should social visitors do next?

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Platform Preferences:** Which social platforms does each persona use?
- **Content Preferences:** What content do they engage with and share?
- **Sharing Behavior:** Which personas are social amplifiers?
- **Social Influence:** Do they have large followings or influence?
- **Engagement Style:** Comments, likes, shares, or passive consumption?
- **Time Patterns:** When are they active on social media?
- **Visual Preferences:** Images, videos, or text content?
- **Community Participation:** Do they join groups or communities?
- **Brand Interaction:** How do they interact with brands on social?
