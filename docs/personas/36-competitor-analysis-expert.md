# Persona: Competitor Analysis Expert

## Profile
- **Name:** David Patel
- **Age:** 39
- **Role:** Competitive Intelligence Manager
- **Company:** Large Professional Services Firm
- **Experience:** 12 years in competitive analysis and market intelligence
- **Location:** Manchester, UK

## Background
David monitors competitors, analyzes their strategies, and identifies competitive threats and opportunities. He regularly reviews competitor websites, marketing materials, and market positioning. His analysis helps his company understand competitive dynamics and differentiate effectively.

## Goals & Motivations
- Track competitor strategies and positioning
- Identify competitive threats and opportunities
- Understand competitor strengths and weaknesses
- Provide actionable competitive intelligence
- Help company differentiate effectively
- Monitor market changes and new entrants

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Competitors who are vague about their offerings
- Difficulty accessing accurate competitive data
- Too much marketing fluff, not enough substance
- Rapidly changing competitive landscape
- Limited budget for competitive intelligence tools
- Separating genuine differentiation from marketing claims

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Services Detail:** Specific information about what's offered
- **Pricing Signals:** Any indication of pricing strategy or positioning
- **Target Market:** Who the company targets and why
- **Value Proposition:** How they position themselves
- **Differentiators:** What makes them unique
- **Case Studies:** Types of clients and problems solved
- **Team & Capabilities:** Size, expertise, locations, specializations
- **Content & Messaging:** Tone, approach, themes
- **Technology Stack:** Any technology or tools they use
- **Partnerships:** Strategic relationships and alliances
- **Client Logos:** Who they work with
- **Awards & Recognition:** Market validation and positioning
- **News & Updates:** Recent developments and direction
- **Job Postings:** Growth areas and capabilities they're building

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Competitive Targeting:** Which personas are competitors targeting?
- **Share of Voice:** Which personas are most competed for?
- **Positioning Differences:** How do competitors position to each persona?
- **Capability Gaps:** Which personas are underserved by competitors?
- **Differentiation Opportunities:** Where can you differentiate by persona?
- **Competitive Threats:** Which competitors are strongest with which personas?
- **Market Whitespace:** Persona needs not addressed by competition?
- **Competitive Moves:** Are competitors shifting persona focus?

## Technical Proficiency
- Expert: Competitive analysis, market research, intelligence gathering
- Advanced: SWOT analysis, positioning analysis, market mapping
- Intermediate: Industry knowledge, strategic analysis
- Tools: Competitor tracking tools, web scrapers, analytics tools, research databases

## Preferred Communication Channels
- Competitive analysis reports
- Market positioning matrices
- SWOT analyses
- Battlecards and competitive briefs
- Intelligence dashboards

## Key Quotes
> "Your website tells me everything about your strategy, whether you intend to or not."

> "I'm looking for substance, not spin. What do you actually do and for whom?"

> "The companies that worry me most are the ones with clear, specific positioning—not the ones trying to be everything to everyone."

## Red Flags for This Persona
- Vague or generic positioning
- No clear target market or specialization
- Missing services or pricing information
- No recent updates or news
- Generic stock content without differentiation
- Copying competitor messaging or positioning
- Weak or no case studies
- No evidence of unique capabilities
- Inconsistent messaging across pages
- No clear value proposition

## Website Evaluation Checklist
- [ ] Is positioning clear and specific?
- [ ] Are services detailed and differentiated?
- [ ] Is target market clearly defined?
- [ ] Are differentiators substantive?
- [ ] Are capabilities and expertise evident?
- [ ] Do case studies show unique value?
- [ ] Is pricing strategy apparent?
- [ ] Are partnerships and alliances mentioned?
- [ ] Is content original, not generic?
- [ ] Are growth areas visible (job postings)?
- [ ] Is there recent news or updates?
- [ ] Can I build a competitive profile easily?
- [ ] Are strengths and weaknesses apparent?
- [ ] Is this a serious competitive threat?
- [ ] What can I learn about their strategy?
