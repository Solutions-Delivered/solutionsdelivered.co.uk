# Persona: Marketing Manager

## Profile
- **Name:** Emma Richardson
- **Age:** 39
- **Role:** Head of Marketing
- **Company Type:** B2B SaaS Company (mid-size)
- **Experience:** 12 years in marketing, 5 years in leadership
- **Location:** Manchester, UK

## Background
Emma leads a marketing team responsible for brand awareness, lead generation, and customer retention. She oversees website strategy, content marketing, campaigns, and demand generation. She's data-driven but also understands the importance of brand storytelling and emotional connection.

## Goals & Motivations
- Increase brand awareness and market share
- Generate high-quality leads for the sales team
- Demonstrate clear ROI on marketing spend
- Build strong brand positioning and messaging
- Create consistent brand experience across all touchpoints
- Support customer retention and advocacy

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Proving marketing ROI to executive leadership
- Aligning website messaging with campaign themes
- Balancing multiple stakeholder demands for website changes
- Limited development resources for website updates
- Difficulty tracking user journey across touchpoints
- Competitive market with similar messaging
- Budget constraints for tools and campaigns

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Clear Value Proposition:** Immediately understandable positioning
- **Lead Capture:** Strategic forms, CTAs, and conversion paths
- **Brand Consistency:** Alignment with brand guidelines and campaign messaging
- **Content Hub:** Blog, resources, case studies to support content marketing
- **Analytics Integration:** Google Analytics, marketing automation, tracking pixels
- **SEO Performance:** Strong organic search visibility
- **Campaign Landing Pages:** Ability to create custom landing pages quickly
- **Social Proof:** Testimonials, case studies, client logos, awards
- **Mobile Experience:** Seamless mobile user experience
- **Page Speed:** Fast loading for better conversion and SEO
- **A/B Testing Capability:** Ability to test different messages and designs
- **Integration:** CRM, marketing automation, email platforms

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Buyer Stages:** Are personas mapped to awareness, consideration, decision stages?
- **Marketing Channels:** Which channels does each persona prefer?
- **Content Preferences:** What content formats resonate with each persona?
- **Pain Points:** What problems can our solution solve for them?
- **Buying Triggers:** What prompts them to seek solutions?
- **Objections:** What concerns or hesitations do they have?
- **Messaging:** What language and tone resonates with them?
- **Targeting:** Can we target them effectively with paid advertising?
- **Customer Journey:** How do they move from awareness to purchase?
- **Lifetime Value:** What's the revenue potential of each persona?

## Technical Proficiency
- Expert: Marketing strategy, brand development, campaign management
- Advanced: Google Analytics, marketing automation (HubSpot, Marketo), CRM systems
- Intermediate: SEO, paid advertising, email marketing, basic HTML/CSS
- Tools: HubSpot, Google Analytics, SEMrush, Hootsuite, Canva

## Preferred Communication Channels
- Campaign briefs and marketing plans
- Brand guidelines and messaging documents
- Analytics dashboards and performance reports
- Competitive analysis
- Customer journey maps

## Key Quotes
> "Our website is our most important marketing asset. It needs to work hard for us."

> "I need personas that help me craft messages that resonate, not just demographic data."

> "If we can't track it, we can't optimize it. Measurement is everything."

## Red Flags for This Persona
- Unclear or weak value proposition
- No clear CTAs or conversion paths
- Poor SEO performance and organic visibility
- Missing analytics or tracking implementation
- Inconsistent brand messaging
- No lead capture mechanisms
- Slow page load times
- Poor mobile experience
- No social proof or credibility signals
- Difficult to update or create landing pages

## Website Evaluation Checklist
- [ ] Is the value proposition immediately clear?
- [ ] Are CTAs strategically placed and compelling?
- [ ] Is branding consistent with other marketing materials?
- [ ] Are analytics and tracking properly implemented?
- [ ] Is the site optimized for SEO?
- [ ] Are there effective lead capture forms?
- [ ] Is social proof prominently displayed?
- [ ] Does the site load quickly?
- [ ] Is the mobile experience excellent?
- [ ] Can landing pages be created easily?
- [ ] Are there clear paths for different personas?
- [ ] Is content optimized for conversion?
