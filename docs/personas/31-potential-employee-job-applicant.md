# Persona: Potential Employee / Job Applicant

## Profile
- **Name:** Jessica Moore
- **Age:** 29
- **Role:** Business Analyst (Currently Employed)
- **Looking For:** New career opportunity with growth potential
- **Experience:** 5 years in business analysis and consulting
- **Location:** London, UK (Open to remote/hybrid)

## Background
Jessica is actively exploring new opportunities. She's researching companies, evaluating culture fit, and looking for roles that offer growth, interesting work, and good work-life balance. She's selective and will thoroughly research potential employers online.

## Goals & Motivations
- Find fulfilling work with interesting challenges
- Advance career and develop new skills
- Work for a company with good culture and values
- Achieve better work-life balance
- Competitive salary and benefits
- Make a positive impact

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Difficulty assessing company culture from outside
- Unclear career progression opportunities
- Concerns about work-life balance
- Uncertainty about day-to-day work reality
- Vague or inflated job descriptions
- Lengthy, complicated application processes
- Lack of transparency about compensation
- Companies that oversell and underdeliver

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Careers Page:** Dedicated section about working at the company
- **Open Positions:** Current job openings with clear descriptions
- **Company Culture:** Authentic glimpse into work environment
- **Employee Testimonials:** Genuine stories from current employees
- **Values & Mission:** Company purpose and principles
- **Benefits & Perks:** Compensation, benefits, work arrangements
- **Career Growth:** Professional development and advancement opportunities
- **Team Information:** Meet the team, leadership bios
- **Work Examples:** Projects and work that shows what you do
- **Application Process:** Clear, straightforward application
- **Diversity & Inclusion:** Commitment to inclusive workplace
- **Office/Remote:** Where and how people work

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Role Alignment:** Does this company have roles matching my skills and interests?
- **Growth Potential:** Can I advance my career here?
- **Culture Fit:** Will I enjoy working with these people?
- **Work-Life Balance:** What are the actual expectations?
- **Compensation:** Is pay competitive for my level?
- **Reputation:** What do others say about working here?
- **Stability:** Is this a stable, growing company?
- **Values Match:** Do company values align with mine?
- **Work Quality:** Will the work be interesting and meaningful?

## Technical Proficiency
- Expert: Job search platforms (LinkedIn, Indeed), research skills
- Advanced: Industry-specific knowledge, professional networking
- Intermediate: Company research, salary research tools
- Tools: LinkedIn, Glassdoor, Indeed, company websites, professional networks

## Preferred Communication Channels
- Company careers page
- LinkedIn company page and jobs
- Glassdoor reviews
- Employee social media posts
- Recruitment emails and outreach
- Job boards

## Key Quotes
> "I can tell within 30 seconds if a company actually cares about its employees or just has good marketing."

> "Show me real employees, real projects, and real culture—not stock photos and buzzwords."

> "If you can't tell me the salary range upfront, I'm not interested."

## Red Flags for This Persona
- No careers page or outdated job listings
- Generic stock photos instead of real employees
- Vague job descriptions full of buzzwords
- No information about culture or values
- Missing salary or benefits information
- Complicated, lengthy application process
- No employee testimonials or stories
- Bad Glassdoor reviews without responses
- No diversity and inclusion information
- Website focused only on selling to customers

## Website Evaluation Checklist
- [ ] Is there a dedicated careers page?
- [ ] Are job openings current and detailed?
- [ ] Is company culture authentically represented?
- [ ] Are there real employee stories?
- [ ] Are values and mission clearly stated?
- [ ] Is benefits information available?
- [ ] Are career growth paths described?
- [ ] Can I see who I'd be working with?
- [ ] Are salary ranges provided?
- [ ] Is the application process straightforward?
- [ ] Is diversity and inclusion addressed?
- [ ] Are work arrangements (remote/hybrid) clear?
- [ ] Can I get a sense of daily work?
- [ ] Are employee reviews monitored and addressed?
- [ ] Does the company seem like a good place to work?
