# Persona: Data Analyst

## Profile
- **Name:** Kevin Murphy
- **Age:** 31
- **Role:** Senior Data Analyst
- **Company Type:** Digital Marketing Agency
- **Experience:** 7 years in data analysis and business intelligence
- **Location:** Dublin, Ireland

## Background
Kevin analyzes website data, user behavior, and business metrics to identify insights and optimization opportunities. He works with Google Analytics, data visualization tools, and various analytics platforms. He values data quality, proper tracking implementation, and actionable insights.

## Goals & Motivations
- Extract actionable insights from data
- Improve data-driven decision making
- Optimize website performance and conversion
- Build reliable data infrastructure
- Communicate insights effectively to stakeholders
- Identify trends and patterns

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Poor data quality or incomplete tracking
- Stakeholders who ignore data insights
- Limited access to necessary data
- Tools that don't integrate well
- Difficulty attributing outcomes to specific changes
- Data privacy regulations affecting tracking
- Explaining complex analysis to non-technical stakeholders

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Analytics Implementation:** Proper Google Analytics or alternatives setup
- **Event Tracking:** Custom events for key interactions
- **Conversion Tracking:** Goal tracking and funnel analysis
- **Data Layer:** Proper data layer for tag management
- **User ID Tracking:** Cross-device and logged-in user tracking
- **E-commerce Tracking:** Transaction and product data (if applicable)
- **A/B Testing Capability:** Experimentation infrastructure
- **Data Quality:** Clean, accurate, consistent data
- **Attribution:** Multi-touch attribution tracking
- **Dashboard Access:** Real-time metrics and KPIs
- **Privacy Compliance:** GDPR-compliant tracking
- **Integration:** Connection to CRM, marketing automation, etc.

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Behavioral Patterns:** How does each persona behave on site?
- **Conversion Paths:** What journeys do personas take to convert?
- **Segment Performance:** Which personas have best metrics?
- **Drop-off Points:** Where do personas abandon processes?
- **Engagement Metrics:** Time on site, pages per session, bounce rate by persona
- **Channel Preferences:** How do personas discover and return to site?
- **Device Usage:** Desktop vs. mobile vs. tablet by persona
- **Geographic Patterns:** Location-based behavior differences
- **Time Patterns:** When do different personas visit?
- **Value Metrics:** Revenue, LTV, or other value per persona

## Technical Proficiency

- Advanced: Data visualization (Tableau, Power BI), A/B testing, tag management
- Intermediate: Python/R for analysis, marketing analytics, CRM systems
- Tools: Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Tableau, SQL databases, Excel

## Red Flags for This Persona
- No analytics tracking or poor implementation
- Missing event tracking for key actions
- No conversion goals configured
- Poor data quality or inconsistent tracking
- No ability to segment users
- Missing integration between systems
- No A/B testing capability
- Privacy-invasive tracking without consent
- Can't connect website data to business outcomes
- No access to necessary data for analysis

- [ ] Is analytics properly implemented?
- [ ] Are key events and interactions tracked?
- [ ] Are conversion goals configured?
- [ ] Is data quality high and consistent?
- [ ] Can users be segmented meaningfully?
- [ ] Is there integration with other systems?
- [ ] Can A/B tests be run?
- [ ] Is tracking GDPR-compliant?
- [ ] Are funnels and paths analyzable?
- [ ] Can attribution be tracked?
- [ ] Are dashboards and reports available?
- [ ] Is cross-device tracking implemented?
