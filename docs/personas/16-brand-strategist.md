# Persona: Brand Strategist

## Profile
- **Name:** Isabella Costa
- **Age:** 37
- **Role:** Brand Strategy Director
- **Company Type:** Brand Strategy Consultancy
- **Experience:** 12 years in brand strategy and positioning
- **Location:** London, UK

## Background
Isabella helps organizations define and evolve their brand identity, positioning, and messaging. She combines market research, competitive analysis, and brand theory to create distinctive brands that resonate with target audiences. She believes great brands are built on truth, consistency, and emotional connection.

## Goals & Motivations
- Create distinctive, memorable brand positioning
- Build emotional connections between brands and audiences
- Ensure consistent brand experience across touchpoints
- Differentiate clients from competitors
- Drive brand equity and long-term value
- Translate brand strategy into actionable guidelines

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Stakeholders with conflicting visions of the brand
- Pressure to follow trends instead of being distinctive
- Difficulty measuring brand impact
- Inconsistent brand execution across touchpoints
- Budget constraints for proper brand research
- Short-term thinking that undermines long-term brand building

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Brand Positioning:** Clear, differentiated positioning statement
- **Brand Personality:** Consistent tone, voice, and character
- **Visual Identity:** Cohesive use of colors, typography, imagery
- **Brand Story:** Compelling narrative about purpose and values
- **Emotional Resonance:** Content that creates emotional connection
- **Consistency:** Aligned messaging across all pages and touchpoints
- **Authenticity:** Genuine representation of company culture and values
- **Differentiation:** Clear distinction from competitors
- **Target Audience Alignment:** Language and design that resonates with intended audience
- **Brand Architecture:** Clear relationship between products, services, and parent brand
- **Cultural Relevance:** Brand that feels current and meaningful
- **Sensory Experience:** Cohesive visual, verbal, and interactive brand experience

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Brand Relationship:** How does each persona relate to or perceive the brand?
- **Emotional Drivers:** What emotions motivate each persona?
- **Values Alignment:** Which personas share the brand's values?
- **Archetype Fit:** Which brand archetypes resonate with each persona?
- **Lifestyle Context:** How does the brand fit into their life?
- **Aspiration:** What does the brand help them become or achieve?
- **Cultural Context:** What cultural factors influence their brand perception?
- **Brand Loyalty:** Which personas are most likely to become brand advocates?
- **Communication Style:** What tone and voice resonates with each persona?
- **Touchpoint Preferences:** Where and how does each persona interact with the brand?

## Red Flags for This Persona
- Inconsistent tone and messaging
- Generic, undifferentiated positioning
- Following competitor trends instead of leading
- Misalignment between brand promise and experience
- Inconsistent visual identity
- Inauthentic or inflated brand claims
- No clear brand personality
- Trying to appeal to everyone
- Conflicting messages across touchpoints
- Visual design that doesn't reflect brand positioning
