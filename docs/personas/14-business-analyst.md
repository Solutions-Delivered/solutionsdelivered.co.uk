# Persona: Business Analyst

## Profile
- **Name:** Olivia Thompson
- **Age:** 35
- **Role:** Senior Business Analyst
- **Company Type:** Enterprise Organization
- **Experience:** 10 years in business analysis and requirements gathering
- **Location:** London, UK

## Background
Olivia bridges the gap between business stakeholders and technical teams. She specializes in gathering requirements, documenting processes, and ensuring solutions meet business objectives. She's analytical, detail-oriented, and focused on measurable outcomes.

## Goals & Motivations
- Define clear, measurable business requirements
- Ensure solutions align with business goals and KPIs
- Document processes and workflows thoroughly
- Identify opportunities for improvement and efficiency
- Demonstrate ROI and business value
- Facilitate communication between business and technical teams

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Vague or conflicting requirements from stakeholders
- Scope creep and changing priorities
- Difficulty quantifying qualitative benefits
- Stakeholders who don't understand technical constraints
- Limited access to data for analysis
- Balancing ideal solutions with practical constraints

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Clear Business Goals:** Alignment between website and business objectives
- **Measurable KPIs:** Defined success metrics and tracking
- **User Flows:** Documented processes and user journeys
- **Requirements Traceability:** Clear connection between requirements and features
- **Data Collection:** Analytics, forms, and feedback mechanisms
- **Business Logic:** Well-defined rules and validation
- **Integration Points:** Clear understanding of system dependencies
- **Scalability:** Ability to grow with business needs
- **ROI Justification:** Clear business case for features and investments
- **Stakeholder Alignment:** Solution that meets multiple stakeholder needs
- **Documentation:** Process flows, requirements docs, technical specs
- **Success Criteria:** Defined acceptance criteria for features

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Business Value:** What's the revenue/cost impact of serving this persona?
- **Volume:** How many users does this persona represent?
- **Strategic Importance:** How critical is this persona to business strategy?
- **Requirements Complexity:** How complex are their needs?
- **Data Availability:** Can we measure and track this persona's behavior?
- **Stakeholder Priority:** Which personas do key stakeholders care about?
- **Resource Requirements:** What investment is needed to serve them well?
- **Competitive Advantage:** Does focusing on this persona differentiate us?
- **Risk Assessment:** What risks are associated with this persona?
- **Dependencies:** How does this persona interact with systems/processes?

> "If you can't measure it, you can't manage it."

> "Requirements without business justification are just wish lists."

## Red Flags for This Persona
- No clear connection to business goals
- Missing or poorly defined KPIs
- No requirements documentation
- Features without clear business justification
- Poor data collection and analytics
- Undefined success criteria
- No stakeholder alignment
- Missing integration specifications
- Unclear business logic or rules
- No consideration of scalability
