# Persona: Industry Analyst

## Profile
- **Name:** Dr. Patricia Coleman
- **Age:** 51
- **Role:** Principal Analyst, Business Consulting Sector
- **Company:** Leading Industry Research Firm
- **Experience:** 25 years analyzing professional services industry
- **Location:** London, UK

## Background
Patricia analyzes trends, competitive landscapes, and market dynamics in the business services sector. She publishes reports, speaks at conferences, and advises organizations. Her assessments influence market perception and can impact business development. She values data, evidence, and substantive insights.

## Goals & Motivations
- Identify emerging trends and market shifts
- Provide accurate, insightful analysis to clients
- Maintain credibility and analytical rigor
- Publish influential research and reports
- Understand competitive dynamics and differentiation
- Forecast market developments

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Companies that overhype or misrepresent capabilities
- Lack of substantive data or evidence
- Difficulty accessing accurate market information
- Too much marketing speak, not enough substance
- Limited time to research all market players
- Pressure to identify truly innovative approaches

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Market Positioning:** Clear articulation of market position
- **Differentiation:** What makes the company unique or innovative
- **Evidence & Data:** Substantive proof of claims (metrics, results, studies)
- **Thought Leadership:** Original insights, not recycled content
- **Client Results:** Quantifiable outcomes and impact
- **Company Information:** Size, growth, leadership, financial stability
- **Service Innovation:** New approaches or methodologies
- **Market Presence:** Awards, recognition, industry participation
- **Transparency:** Honest representation of capabilities and limitations
- **Trend Awareness:** Understanding of market trends and shifts
- **Research & Reports:** Original research or contributed insights
- **Press & Media:** Company news and media coverage

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Market Segmentation:** Which market segments does each persona represent?
- **Buying Behavior:** What are the purchasing patterns and trends?
- **Decision Factors:** What drives decisions in different segments?
- **Market Size:** How large is each persona segment?
- **Growth Trends:** Which segments are growing or declining?
- **Competitive Dynamics:** How do competitors target each persona?
- **Innovation Adoption:** Which personas adopt new approaches?
- **Industry Influence:** Which personas drive market trends?

## Technical Proficiency
- Expert: Market analysis, competitive intelligence, trend forecasting, research methods
- Advanced: Data analysis, industry knowledge, report writing
- Intermediate: Business strategy, marketing concepts
- Tools: Research databases, data analysis tools, industry publications

## Preferred Communication Channels
- Industry reports and white papers
- Research data and market studies
- Press releases and company announcements
- Conference presentations
- Analyst briefings

## Key Quotes
> "Show me the evidence. I'm not interested in aspirational marketing claims."

> "Every company says they're innovative and customer-focused. Prove it with data and differentiation."

> "I can tell within minutes whether a company understands their market or is just following trends."

## Red Flags for This Persona
- Vague, unsubstantiated claims
- No quantifiable results or outcomes
- Generic positioning without differentiation
- Missing or limited company information
- No original thought leadership
- Overhyped marketing without substance
- Lack of transparency about capabilities
- No evidence of market understanding
- Missing press or media coverage
- Ignoring industry trends and shifts

- [ ] Is market positioning clearly articulated?
- [ ] Is differentiation substantive and provable?
- [ ] Are claims backed by evidence and data?
- [ ] Is thought leadership original and insightful?
- [ ] Are client results quantified?
- [ ] Is company information comprehensive?

- [ ] Is market presence and recognition shown?

- [ ] Is there original research or insights?
- [ ] Is press and media coverage available?
