# Persona: SEO Specialist

## Profile
- **Name:** Hannah Wilson
- **Age:** 30
- **Role:** SEO Manager
- **Company Type:** Digital Marketing Agency
- **Experience:** 7 years in SEO and organic search
- **Location:** Leeds, UK

## Background
Hannah specializes in improving organic search visibility through technical SEO, content optimization, and link building. She stays current with algorithm updates and search engine guidelines. She focuses on sustainable, white-hat SEO practices that drive real business results.

## Goals & Motivations
- Increase organic search traffic and rankings
- Improve search visibility for target keywords
- Drive qualified traffic that converts
- Implement SEO best practices
- Stay ahead of algorithm changes
- Demonstrate SEO ROI to stakeholders

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Algorithm updates that impact rankings
- Technical issues that block search engines
- Content that's written without SEO consideration
- Competing with high-authority domains
- Limited development resources for technical fixes
- Stakeholders who expect immediate SEO results
- Black-hat SEO tactics from competitors

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Crawlability:** Clean site structure, proper robots.txt, XML sitemap
- **Indexability:** Pages that search engines can index without barriers
- **Keyword Targeting:** Strategic use of target keywords in content
- **Title Tags & Meta Descriptions:** Optimized for CTR and keywords
- **Heading Structure:** Proper H1, H2, H3 hierarchy with keywords
- **Internal Linking:** Strategic links between related content
- **Mobile-First:** Excellent mobile experience (Google mobile-first indexing)
- **Page Speed:** Fast Core Web Vitals scores
- **Structured Data:** Schema markup for rich snippets
- **Quality Content:** Comprehensive, valuable content that satisfies search intent
- **Backlink Profile:** Quality inbound links from authoritative sites
- **HTTPS:** Secure site with SSL certificate
- **URL Structure:** Clean, descriptive, keyword-rich URLs
- **Image Optimization:** Alt tags, file names, compression

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Search Behavior:** What keywords and phrases do they use?
- **Search Intent:** Informational, navigational, commercial, transactional?
- **Search Volume:** How many people search for persona-related terms?
- **Keyword Difficulty:** How competitive are their target keywords?
- **SERP Features:** What SERP features (featured snippets, local pack) appear?
- **Content Needs:** What content would rank for their searches?
- **Seasonal Trends:** Are there seasonal search patterns?
- **Local vs. National:** Geographic search patterns?
- **Voice Search:** Do they use voice search differently?
- **Related Searches:** What related queries do they make?

## Red Flags for This Persona
- Duplicate content issues
- Slow page load times / poor Core Web Vitals
- Missing or poorly optimized title tags and meta descriptions
- No XML sitemap or robots.txt
- Broken internal links or 404 errors
- Thin or low-quality content
- Missing schema markup
- Not mobile-friendly
- No HTTPS / security issues
- Orphaned pages with no internal links
- Poor heading structure
- Missing alt text on images
