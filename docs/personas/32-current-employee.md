# Persona: Current Employee

## Profile
- **Name:** Marcus Powell
- **Age:** 35
- **Role:** Senior Consultant
- **Tenure:** 3 years with the company
- **Location:** Birmingham, UK

## Background
Marcus is a current employee who uses the website as a reference and resource. He also represents the company to clients and in his network. He's invested in the company's success and reputation. He wants to feel proud of where he works.

## Goals & Motivations
- Access internal resources and information
- Stay informed about company updates
- Feel proud to represent the company
- Share company content with clients and network
- Understand company direction and strategy
- See career growth opportunities

## Pain Points & Challenges
- Difficulty finding internal resources on website
- Website doesn't reflect internal reality
- Outdated information that makes company look bad
- Can't easily share relevant content with clients
- Website focused only on recruiting/selling, not supporting employees
- Missing resources that would help do job better
- Inconsistency between external brand and internal culture

## What They Look For in a Website
- **Company News:** Updates, achievements, company direction
- **Resources:** Client-facing materials they can share
- **Team Directory:** Contact information for colleagues
- **Internal Portal:** Access to employee resources (if applicable)
- **Case Studies:** Success stories to share with clients
- **Company Information:** Accurate representation of culture and capabilities
- **Career Opportunities:** Internal mobility and growth options
- **Events:** Company events, training, professional development
- **Brand Assets:** Logos, presentations, templates
- **Thought Leadership:** Content they can share professionally
- **Pride of Association:** Content that makes them proud to work there
- **Accuracy:** Information that matches their lived experience

## How They Evaluate Personas
- **Employee Representation:** Are current employees authentically represented?
- **Internal Needs:** Does the website serve employee needs?
- **Brand Alignment:** Does external brand match internal culture?
- **Resource Access:** Can employees find what they need?
- **Pride Factor:** Would they share this with their network?
- **Career Development:** Are growth opportunities visible?
- **Company Updates:** Is information current and relevant?
- **Client Sharing:** Can they confidently share content with clients?

## Technical Proficiency
- Expert: Company systems and processes, industry knowledge
- Advanced: Professional tools, client management
- Intermediate: Content sharing, social media professional use
- Tools: Company intranet, CRM, professional software, LinkedIn

## Preferred Communication Channels
- Company intranet
- Internal email communications
- Company website
- Team meetings and all-hands
- Internal messaging (Slack, Teams)

## Key Quotes
> "I need to find case studies and resources quickly when I'm talking to clients."

> "The website should reflect the company I actually work for, not an idealized version."

> "I want to feel proud when I share our website with my network."

## Red Flags for This Persona
- Outdated or inaccurate information
- No employee-specific resources or portal
- Website that doesn't match internal culture
- Missing or hard-to-find case studies
- No company news or updates
- Generic content that doesn't help them
- Inaccessible brand assets or templates
- No career development information
- Website focused only on external audiences
- Content they're embarrassed to share

## Website Evaluation Checklist
- [ ] Is information accurate and current?
- [ ] Are there resources employees can use?
- [ ] Can employees access what they need?
- [ ] Does the site reflect actual company culture?
- [ ] Are case studies easy to find and share?
- [ ] Is company news and updates visible?
- [ ] Are brand assets accessible?
- [ ] Are career opportunities internal posted?
- [ ] Can employees feel proud of the website?
- [ ] Is there content worth sharing professionally?
- [ ] Are team and leadership accurately represented?
- [ ] Is there an employee portal or resources section?
